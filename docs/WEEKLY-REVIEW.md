## Recovery Protocol

If energy scores drop below 5 for two consecutive weeks:

1. **Take a full weekend off.** No coding, no planning, no
   D8X thoughts. Do CrossFit, cook, see friends, anything
   unrelated.

2. **Identify the source:** Is it the work itself (wrong
   priorities), the pace (burning out), or external (sleep,
   relationships, health)?

3. **Cut scope, not corners.** Find 1-2 things you can defer
   to next month without affecting the 6-month goal. Put
   them in a `/docs/LATER.md` file.

4. **Talk to someone.** Not Claude. A human. Spouse, friend,
   fellow founder, therapist. Say the thing out loud.

5. **Review this log.** Three months of entries often reveal
   patterns you can't see day-to-day. "I've been stuck on
   D1 parser improvements for 4 weeks" is information.

6. **Remember the goal.** You're not building D8X because
   you love React. You're building it to build wealth and
   do meaningful work. If the work stops serving those goals,
   something needs to change.
